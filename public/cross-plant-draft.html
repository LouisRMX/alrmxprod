Design notes / open questions

  • Gap vs target, not absolute: Values shown are vs each plant's own target — normalizes for delivery radius differences.
  • Color bands: Green = within target, Amber = 1–30% over, Red = >30% over target.
  • Manager view: Only own plant + portfolio avg — not individual competitor plants.
  • Stale data warning: Assessment older than 90 days shows date in orange.
  • CTA framing: "See what [best plant] does differently" — not "audit why X underperforms".
  • Open question: Should the CTA send an email, open a chat, or link to best plant's report?
  • Open question: Should plants with incomplete assessments be hidden or shown greyed out?
